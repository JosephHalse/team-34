Welcome to NTT Data Smart Glasses Augmented Reality Application

By using the state of the art Epsom Smart Glasses linked to our AR app programme, a user can interact with what they see around them. The glasses will recognise items which they see in front of them, being able to identify different pieces of specialist equipment and show the user information about them. This will enable a technician to access all the information they need, hands free while working, by looking at text displayed on the glasses screen where it is needed and easiest to reach.
