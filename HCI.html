HCI

Design Principles

Our design principles are very simple, we wanted to make our app as efficent as possible. We opted for a simple interface with minimal features so that it would be quick to navigate. We also tried to make it intuitive with the button turning green when object recognition was turned on and red when object recognition was turned off.
